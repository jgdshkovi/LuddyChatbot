The University of Delaware's full-time in-person master's in cybersecurity degree is two years long. It requires students to choose a concentration in software, systems, AI and analytics, or management. Three letters of recommendation are needed, but no standardized test scores are required to submit—minus TOEFL scores from international students. The deadline for the fall semester is August 1. The average undergraduate GPA of 2023–24 enrollees was 3.20.

The University of Delaware's full- and part-time master's degree in data science is 21 months long and thesis optional. On average, there are around 25 students per core course, with program entrants having an average of about two years of prior work experience. The deadline for the fall semester is February 1.
