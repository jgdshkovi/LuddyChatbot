DePaul University’s in-person master’s in cybersecurity program has won more than ten Illinois and Midwest Cyber Collegiate Defense Competitions (CCDC) over the past 15 years. The program also placed second and third in the National CCDC competition within the past five years. The program takes two years to complete, and letters of recommendation and GRE scores are optional for applicants. International students are required to take English proficiency exams. On average, 29 students are enrolled per core cybersecurity course. The application deadline for the fall semester is August 1, and tuition totals approximately $46,000. The average undergraduate GPA of 2023–24 enrollees was 3.33.
