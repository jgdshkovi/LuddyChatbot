The University of Minnesota, Twin Cities's in-person master's in computer science program is full-time and takes about two years to complete. GRE scores will not be reviewed as part of the application process, but two letters of recommendation are needed. Students can focus in one of eight areas, including machine learning, robotics, and computing systems. During the program, students gain real-world experience and go on to careers in industries like big tech, medicine, and finance.
