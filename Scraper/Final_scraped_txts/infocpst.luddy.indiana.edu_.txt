Options
There are four options for completing the casptone requirement in informatics. If you have questions about the options or in general, contact your advisor. You can complete your capstone project in one of the following ways.
Course
INFO I494/I495 (Fall/Spring)
Internship
INFO I491
Serve IT (Internship)
INFO I489 (must apply)
Thesis
INFO I492/493
Please make sure to follow the information in your specific bulletin, as it is what you are bound by for degree completion.
