The University of Chicago offers its online master's in applied data science with full- and part-time options. Applicants are recommended to have 2–3 years of prior work experience and are required to submit two letters of recommendation. International students must demonstrate English proficiency through either TOEFL or IELTS scores. However, standardized testing is not required. The program's graduating class for 2022–2023 comprised just four students. Graduates have gone on to work at companies such as Amazon, McKinsey, BCG, Google, NVIDIA, Argonne Labs, Goldman Sachs, and Proctor & Gamble. The application deadline for the fall semester is June 19.
