Transportation
How to get where you need to go
Whether you need to get from the Indianapolis airport to Bloomington or just across campus, there are many ways to reach your destination.
Traveling from Indianapolis to Bloomington
If you arrive at the Indianapolis airport, it’s easy to get to Bloomington and the IU campus—you can simply take the airport shuttle.
Learn about the airport shuttle
Getting around campus and Bloomington
You can get anywhere you need to go on campus by foot or bicycle. This is how most students go to class.
If you plan to live off campus, you will need to decide what to do about transportation. Some students choose to buy or lease a car, while others get around primarily by bicycle or bus.

Driving in the United States
If you plan to drive while you are in the United States, you will need a driver’s license. International students have two options—the International Driver’s permit (IDP) or an Indiana driver’s license.
The process of getting a license to drive in the U.S. can be complicated.
