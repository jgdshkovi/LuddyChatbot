Oklahoma State University's online master's program in data science is designed to be completed in 33 credits at a cost of $530 per credit. Applicants are required to submit three letters of recommendation, as well as GRE or GMAT scores. However, the program may waive this requirement for those with over five years of relevant work experience. International applicants need to demonstrate proficiency in English with a minimum score of 79 on the TOEFL iBT or 6.5 on the IELTS academic stream. The class of 2023–2024 had an average of 7.75 years of work experience before enrolling. The deadline to apply for the fall term is June 1.

Oklahoma State University's full-time master's in business analytics and data science program lasts 21–24 months, offering internships and opportunities to compete and present in national competitions and conferences. Students can specialize in marketing, health, or cybersecurity analytics. Applicants must submit three letters of recommendation and GMAT or GRE scores, and international students need TOEFL or IELTS scores. Core courses average 40 students, with entrants typically having over two years of work experience. The fall application deadline is March 1, with a priority deadline of January 15.
