Corporate and Foundation Funding
Private companies and foundations are another valuable source of funding for research and innovative activities at Indiana University. IU has long-standing relationships with numerous corporate and foundation supporters and is constantly at work to develop new partnerships. Experts in
Corporate and Foundation Relations
are available to help you in numerous ways as you pursue private funding.
Some organizations have requested coordination through the business partnerships and foundation relations teams, including those on the
critical contact list
.
