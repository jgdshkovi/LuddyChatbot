BUS-M 300 Introduction to Marketing
16 weeks
3 credits
Prerequisite(s)
:
Must not be a Kelley School of Business major
Offered for students minoring in business. Examination of the market economy and marketing institutions in the United States. Decision making and planning from the manager’s point of view and impact of marketing actions from consumer’s point of view.
